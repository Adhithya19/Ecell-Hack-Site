The Problem
Migration is broken.
Here's why.
🗺️

No service map exists

GCP and AWS use fundamentally different service models. Compute Engine ≠ EC2. BigQuery ≠ Redshift. Every mapping requires deep expertise in both platforms simultaneously.

⚠️

Hidden risks, late discovery

IAM model incompatibilities, unsupported configurations, and networking gaps are only found in production. Months into a migration, after millions have been spent.

💸

Consultant-dependent

6–18 month timelines. Millions in fees. Manual spreadsheets and whiteboard sessions. The process hasn't changed in a decade and it's costing organizations dearly.

What We Built

01 — MAPPING ENGINE

Intelligent Service Translation

Upload your Terraform HCL, Deployment Manager YAML, or describe your GCP infra in plain text. Our AI maps every service, configuration, and parameter to its closest AWS equivalent with reasoning for each decision.

02 — RISK DETECTION

Catch IAM incompatibilities, unsupported service configurations, networking topology gaps, and data residency issues before they hit production. Every flag comes with a recommended remediation path.
